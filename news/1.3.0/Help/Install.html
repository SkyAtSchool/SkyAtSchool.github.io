Installation

To install Bunker Browser, go to this this GitHub repository.

You will end up on a page that looks like this:

Click the text that says releases or the latest release on the right sidebar.

You will then end up on this page.

Click the file that says Bunker Browser and ends in a .zip.

Let the file finish downloading, and you will have Bunker Browser on your device.

To open Bunker Browser, follow one of these guides specifically for your device by clicking on them.

Launch (Chromebook)

To launch Bunker Browser, click the circle on the bottom left of your taskbar. It will bring up a small context menu with a search bar.

Click the up arrow and then Files.

Click Recent (or Downloads and My files) and find Bunker-Browser-vXXX-XXX-X.

Right click (or tap both fingers on a trackpad) and click Extract all.

Find the unzipped folder for Bunker Browser and then open it and click "index.html"

It will open Bunker Browser and you'll be done!

All that's left to do is bookmark it (if you want) and explore!
